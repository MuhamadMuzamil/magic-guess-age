About This Game

This is a fun math trick game that magically reveals your age and birth month. Built with ❤️ in DG Khan by Muzamil Kareem.

How the magic works:

1. You multiply your age by 5: (Age × 5)

2. Add 8: (Age × 5 + 8)

3. Multiply by 2: (Age × 10 + 16)

4. You tell me this number and your birth month

5. I subtract your birth month and then divide by 10 to get your age!

Formula: ((Final Number - Birth Month) - 16) ÷ 10 = Age
